## Create a Diversion

**Circumstance**: You make a **Leadership** Gambit to draw the attention of a group
**Effort Cost**: 2
**Effect**: Gain **+1 Boon** on the roll. On a Success, all opposition rolls involving
NPCs are at **+1 Bane** until the end of the scene as long as you do not
take any additional Gambits or use Proactive abilities. On a Critical Success,
your allies' Gambits are at **+1 Boon** as well and you refund 1 Effort.

## Intimidating Presence

**Circumstance**: You or an ally attempt a Gambit where Infamy would be added
to the Opposition roll
**Uses/Cooldown**: 1/Arc per Faction
**Effect**: Add Infamy to the player roll instead  
**Upgrades:**


## Natural Charisma

**Circumstance**: You succeed on any Gambit involving social interaction
**Uses/Cooldown**: 1/Episode
**Effect**: The party gains +1 Eunoia
