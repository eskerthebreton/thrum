## Silent Pursuit

**Circumstance**: You attempt a Gambit to follow someone undetected
**Effort Cost**: 1
**Effect**: Choose 1: Gain **+2 Boons**, or the Gambit becomes
**Powerful** and loses **Risky**  
**Upgrades:**


## Now You Don't

**Uses/Cooldown**: 1/Episode
**Effect**: You can apply the **Intercede** ability to your own Stealth Gambit
without spending Effort
**Upgrades:**


## I Am No One

**Effect**: You have **+1 Boon** on Stealth Gambits you make in any scene
where the party is outnumbered by nonhostile NPCs
**Upgrades:**


## Cover of Darkness

**Effect**: Your Stealth Gambits are always **Controlled** when in darkness
**Upgrades:**


## Cover Their Tracks

**Success**: Your Stealth Gambits made to reach a location undetected apply
to the whole party provided the location is known when this
activity is taken
**Upgrades:**


## Master of Disguise

**Success**: Designate a faction. You disguise the party as generic members of that
faction while this effect persists. The party's own renown and infamy
do not apply to Gambits while this effect is active, and are replaced
by modifiers appropriate to relationships with the designated faction.
Any Eunoia earned while this effect is active must be expended before it ends.
**Upgrades:**
