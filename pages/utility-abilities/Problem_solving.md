## Deus Ex Machina

**Circumstance**: You or an ally attempts to do something Risky against formidable opposition.
**Uses/Cooldown**: 1/Arc
**Effect**: Swap the player and opposition modifiers, and invert the Risk profile:
  **Risky** becomes **Powerful** and vice versa,
  **Costly** becomes **Controlled** and vice versa, and 
  if there are Boons or Banes in effect, swap those as well.

## Eureka Moment

**Circumstance**: A Gambit attempt by you or an ally results in new information
**Uses/Cooldown**: 1/Episode
**Effect**: The GM offers additional detail or a second related piece of information
relevant to the party's current Arc objective
**Upgrades:**


## Puzzle Enthusiast

**Effect**: Your **Problem Solving** Gambits involving Traps, Ciphers, Riddles,
and Puzzles are at **+1 Boon** and are **Powerful**
**Upgrades:**


## Student of People

**Effect**: When the party interacts with an NPC that has an ulterior motive
or has something to hide, you intuit this (but you do not necessarily
learn what it is). When a piece of information offered by an NPC contradicts
another (either from the same NPC or a different one), you notice this as well.
**Upgrades:**


## Mental Conditioning

**Success**: You gain **+1 Boon** on **Research** and **Reconnaissance**
**Upgrades:**


## Strategize

**Success**: You may present the GM with up to two sets of two alternatives
involving the current or upcoming Arc objective. The GM offers
any relevant pros and cons that make use of information or context
the party could reasonably have had access to.
