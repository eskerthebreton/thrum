## Quickfingered

**Circumstance**: You attempt a Gambit that involves an act of time-sensitive
manual precision, such as stitching a wound, picking a pocket,
manipulating a deck of cards, or playing a musical instrument
**Effort Cost**: 1
**Effect**: You can make the Gambit **Controlled** or **Powerful**. You can use this
modifier twice on the same Gambit to add both properties.  
**Upgrades:**


## Do It With Flare

**Circumstance**: You get a critical success on a Powerful Gambit using Craft
in front of NPC witnesses
**Uses/Cooldown**: 1/Episode
**Effect**: The party gains +1 Eunoia
**Upgrades:**


## Mechanically-Minded

**Effect**: When you attempt a Gambit to understand or intervene in the operation
of something with moving parts (such as a machine or lock),
it is **Controlled** and **Powerful**  
**Upgrades:**


## Utility Belt

**Effect**: At the start of each episode, you may select an Ability from another Art.
You cannot repeat the same one more than once per Arc.
**Upgrades:**


## Assistive Technology

**Success**: Choose a Utility Art. You fashion a rough device that aids in that pursuit.
Your own Gambits using that art become **Controlled**, and you may
use the **Help** ability on Gambits using that Art without expending Effort,
as long as this Buff is active. When a Gambit benefitting from this Buff is a
Critical Fumble, the device breaks and this Buff ends.  
**Upgrades:**
