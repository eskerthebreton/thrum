## Shrewd Negotiation

**Circumstance**: You attempt a Gambit to improve the terms of a deal, such as
a quest reward or a purchase price
**Effort Cost**: 1
**Effect**: The Gambit becomes **Controlled**

## Silver Tongue

**Circumstance**: You or an ally attempt any Gambit involving social interaction
**Uses/Cooldown**: 1/Episode
**Effect**: Add the party's current Eunoia total to the roll  
**Upgrades:**


## Smooth Operator

**Effect**: When you use **Intercede** to mitigate or prevent consequences
of a social nature, it costs only 1 Effort
**Upgrades:**


## Charlatan

**Effect**: Opposition rolls on Gambits made to deceive an individual NPC
are at **+1 Bane**, provided you have not previously failed
at such a gambit with that NPC
**Upgrades:**


## Diplomacy

**Success**: Designate a Faction. The party temporarily gains **+1 Renown**,
**+1 Infamy** or **-1 Infamy** with that faction (reverted when
this Buff ends)
**Upgrades:**


## Extended Stay

**Success**: If any ally failed a Downtime Activity roll during this instance
of downtime, they can attempt a different Downtime Activity  
**Special**: This activity can be declared and resolved after everyone else has
attemped their Downtime Activity
