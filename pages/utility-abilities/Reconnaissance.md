## Clairvoyant Synesthesia

**Circumstance**: You attempt a gambit to see, hear, or smell something in a nearby location
**Effort Cost**: 1
**Effect**: If the Gambit succeeds, you obtain information from all of your senses
as though you were up close
**Upgrades:**


## Retroactive Continuity

**Circumstance**: You or an ally fumbles a Gambit opposed by an NPC or in a location
the party has interacted with in a previous episode
**Uses/Cooldown**: 1/Episode
**Effect**: You recall something you noticed on a previous occasion that suggests
a different approach. You or your ally may treat the first attempt
as if it never happened, and reattempt the Gambit with a different
Approach, at +1 Boon.
**Upgrades:**


## Eagle-Eye

**Effect**: Whenever you are outdoors and you make a Reconnassaince Gambit
to observe something in the distance, choose one of the following:\n
* The Opposition is treated as if you were half the distance away
* It becomes **Powerful**
**Upgrades:**


## Forensic Specialist

**Effect**: Your Gambits made to notice clues about past activities in a location
(such as finding footprints, smudges or other marks, or evidence of
something out of place) are at **+1 Boon**
**Upgrades:**


## Due Diligence

**Success**: You learn a piece of useful information about your current or next
Arc objective. Choose one of the following:\n
* You overhear something about a specific NPC. The party gains one of **+1 Boon**,
  **Controlled** or **Powerful** on **Influence** Gambits involving them (based on
  the nature of the information)
* You discover a hidden route or entrance to a specified location. Fill in one
  segment on a Progress Clock involving reaching that location (representing a
  more efficient path), 
  or add one to an associated Threat Clock (representing a safer one).
* You gain advance knowledge of an obstacle or hazard. When you or an ally
  attempts any Gambit to overcome it, it loses the **Risky** property if it
  had it; otherwise, it becomes **Controlled**.
**Upgrades:**
