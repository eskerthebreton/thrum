## Historical Aside

**Circumstance**: You or an ally attempt a Gambit involving a public figure, institution, or place
**Uses/Cooldown**: 1/Episode
**Effect**: You remember reading something that could help here! The Gambit gains
**+1 Boon** and is **Powerful**
**Upgrades:**


## Applied Booksmarts

**Circumstance**: An ally makes any Gambit
**Uses/Cooldown**: 1/Scene
**Effect**: You may make a **Research** roll. On a success, the Gambit gains
**+1 Boon**. On a Fumble, there is no effect. On a Critical Fumble,
the Gambit gains **+1 Bane**.
**Upgrades:**


## Polymath

**Effect**: When you gain this Ability, designate a Utility Art other than this one.
Gain +1 to your modifier in that Art  
**Special**: You can choose this ability more than once, but must choose a different Art each time
**Upgrades:**


## Specialist

**Effect**: When you gain this Ability, choose one of the following areas of expertise.
Your **Research** Gambits made to recall or acquire knowledge in that area
are **Powerful**\n
* Botany and Brewing
* The Arcane Arts
* Human Physiology and Medicine
* Religious or Occult Practices    
**Special**: You may choose this ability more than once
**Upgrades:**


## Anthropological Staycation

**Success**: Designate a Faction. You brush up on their history, customs and practices
that provides a benefit when interacting with a member of that Faction socially,
or to interact with a location they control. During such an interaction,
choose one of the following:\n
* You may make a Gambit **Controlled** or **Powerful**. If the Gambit is fumbled, end this effect.
* You may use **Intercede** on the Gambit without expending Effort. Then end this effect.
**Upgrades:**


## Crash Course

**Success**: Choose one Utility Art. You may use **Help** on Gambits involving
that Art without expending effort. When you do, you grant
**+2 Boons** instead of +1.
**Upgrades:**
