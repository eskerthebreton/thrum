## Messenger of Woe

**Potential**: You inflict one or more stacks of any Status (up to once per turn)
**Cost**: 4
**Target**: Self
**Effect**: Once per turn immediately before or after your Action (including this one) you can
**Teleport** up to 3 and choose **Burning**, **Slowed**, **Blinded** or **Shocked**.
Deal **+1** of the chosen status as an Effect to any enemy adjacent to the space you
left or arrived.
**Upgrades:**
    a. Reduce the Cost by 1
    b. You can use the Effect before and after your Action
