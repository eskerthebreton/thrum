## Cannonball

**Potential**: You are targeted with an attack
**Cost**: 6
**Target**: Aura 6
**Effect**: Teleport up to your Speed, then all enemies in the Aura are pulled toward you
as far as possible
**Success**: Affected enemies are **+1 Weakened** and **+1 Slowed**
**Critical**: Deal **Fatigue**
**Upgrades:**
    a. On a Critical, the target is **Stunned**
    b. Deal **Fatigue** on a Success
