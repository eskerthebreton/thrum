## Twirling Towards Freedom

**Potential**: An enemy is moved as a result of one of your abilities
(not including this one)
**Cost**: 4
**Target**: Aura $\infty$
**Effect**: You may move each character in the Aura a number of spaces equal to
their distance from you either clockwise or counterclockwise along
the square of spaces that distance from you. You can choose different
directions for each character. Enemies who collide with another character
or with terrain which is +2 or more from their previous space stop
moving and are **Stunned**
**Upgrades:**
    a. Enemies who collide also take **Fatigue**
    b. When you move an enemy using this effect, you may move them 1 space diagonally
at any point during their path such that they end up 1 space further from you
than they started
